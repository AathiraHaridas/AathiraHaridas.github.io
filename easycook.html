User Personas & Research
03

The users can browse the recipes anytime and save them to view it later. They may use it at home before cooking or when taking a break at home.

WHEN

The product will offer users to browse through the recipes, search in the database and online and save the recipe. The users can browse recipes through ethnicity and join a community. The recipe page will have features to read the recipes  and follow the recipe in an organized manner.

WHAT

Users that cook daily or looking to cook something new. This also applies to people who are interested in cooking and trying to learn cooking.

WHO
02

There are many recipe websites online, but to reach the original recipe directly, the user has to go through a lot of stories and written description. Because of the length of the page, some of these recipe forget the simple features like some mesurements, certain instructions or a video recipe. Thus making the user handicapped to attain the desired result.

Problem
Quick & easy to follow recipes tailore-made for you!
UI/UX Design Project
EasyCook


“As a user, I want the recipes to be categorised based on country of origin, so that I can choose which cuisine I want to try, each day.”

“As a user, I want to have a personal cookbook, so I can save all the recipes I like, including the ones I am yet to discover.”

“As a user, I want to see my recipes in a proper order, with the steps mentioned separately, so I don’t get confused what to do when and how.”

“As a user, when I watch a video recipe, I want to see the written recipes, so that I follow what ingredients are used and what quantity needs to be added.”

“As a user, I want to see the Chef’s name and photograph, so that I can choose their recipes.”

“As a user, I want to see meal preparation suggestions of differnet cuisines, so that I don’t waste time procrastinating about what I should I cook in a day.”
